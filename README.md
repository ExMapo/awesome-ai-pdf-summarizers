There are hundreds of tools claiming to summarize PDFs using AI, but most lists are outdated or unstructured.

* Save time reading long PDFs
* Extract key ideas quickly
* Improve research and study workflows
* Convert documents into structured knowledge

## General AI PDF Summarizers

* [MindMap AI PDF Summarizer](https://mindmapai.app/pdf-summarizer) — Converts PDFs into structured and visual summaries.
* [PDFCandy PDF Summarizer](https://pdfcandy.com/pdf-summarizer.html) — Simple browser-based summarization tool.
* [QuillBot](https://quillbot.com/) — AI writing assistant with summarization features.
* [Jotform AI PDF Summarizer](https://www.jotform.com/ai/summarizer/pdf/) — Easy-to-use summarizer with clean UI.
